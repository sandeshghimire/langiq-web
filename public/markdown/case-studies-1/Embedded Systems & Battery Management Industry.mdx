## Introduction to the Embedded Systems & Battery Management Industry

The embedded systems and battery management industry represents one of the most critical sectors in modern technology, powering everything from electric vehicles and drones to industrial equipment and consumer electronics. Companies like **EmbeddedOne** specialize in developing sophisticated Battery Management Systems (BMS) that ensure the safe, efficient, and optimal operation of lithium-ion battery packs across diverse applications.

EmbeddedOne's expertise spans both low voltage systems (under 80V) for applications like 12V starter batteries and 48V auxiliary power systems, as well as high voltage systems up to 1500V for electric vehicles and industrial applications. Their comprehensive engineering services include hardware, software, and firmware development with strict adherence to automotive-grade ECU development standards, functional safety requirements (ISO-26262), and UL deliverables.

The company serves multiple sectors including automotive, marine, aerial, and consumer electronics, providing scalable BMS solutions for applications ranging from power tools and recreational vehicles to electric vehicles, eVTOL aircraft, and industrial systems. Their focus on USA-made products and direct partnerships with semiconductor leaders like Microchip and NXP positions them uniquely in the competitive embedded systems landscape.
